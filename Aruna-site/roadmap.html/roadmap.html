AI Learning Roadmap

Your Journey from Beginner to AI Engineer

Step 1: Learn Python

Python is the most popular programming language for AI and Machine Learning.

Step 2: Learn Mathematics

Focus on Statistics, Probability, Algebra, and Calculus basics.

Step 3: Learn Machine Learning

Understand supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning.

Step 4: Learn Deep Learning

Study neural networks, CNNs, RNNs, and transformers.

Step 5: Learn NLP & Computer Vision

Build AI systems that understand language and images.

Step 6: Build Projects

Create chatbots, image classifiers, recommendation systems, and AI apps.

Step 7: Become an AI Engineer

Master AI tools, deployment, cloud services, and real-world applications.
